# Proverbs 31 — elegant daily devotional app

A calm, premium Christian devotional app built around a curated list of verses you'll provide. Every screen uses your plum / gold / deep-teal / cream palette with serif headers and soft, airy spacing.

**Visual Design**

- Warm cream background (#F8F5EE) with plum (#A28CA1) primary and gold (#C2A47E) accents
- Elegant serif headers paired with a clean sans-serif body
- Rounded cards (16–20px), soft shadows, no harsh borders
- Subtle tap animations and smooth transitions throughout
- A beautifully designed app icon: cream background with a gold serif "P" crowned by a delicate plum laurel, feeling timeless and feminine

**Home — Daily Verse**

- Heart icon (left), "Proverbs 31" serif title (center), menu icon (right)
- Hero verse card: small gold "VERSE OF THE DAY" label, large centered scripture, reference below
- "Verse Commentary" section with a readable long-form paragraph, and a gold audio button that reads the verse aloud using text-to-speech
- Engagement row: views, likes, comments, share — active state glows plum/gold
- Inline comment box for quick reflections

**Devotionals**

- Hero verse card with bible verse centered with scripture and reference below
- "Devotional" section with a readable long-form paragraph, (title, short or long excerpt) one devotional per day
- Detail screen with elegant typography and a save-to-favorites action

**Favorites**

- Clean list of saved verses and devotionals with swipe-to-remove
- Empty state with a gentle gold illustration and encouraging copy

**Journal**

- List of journal entries with date and preview (saved on device, persists between sessions)
- Compose screen with a soft cream paper feel, title and body fields, optional verse tag

**More Info (Settings)**

- "Daily Notifications" toggle
- Support Us section with plum buttons: Rate This App, More Apps
- About Us section with lighter buttons: Facebook Like Us, Follow Us On Instagram, Join Our Community, Contact Us

**Bottom Navigation**

- Four tabs — Verse, Devotionals, Favorites, Journal — plum when active, muted gold when inactive

**Content**

- Once approved, please share the .plist of verses and I'll wire them in directly. Until then I'll scaffold with a small placeholder list using the same structure so the app runs beautifully, and swap in your real data as soon as you provide it.

**Interactions & polish**

- Text-to-speech playback for the daily verse with play/pause state
- Haptic feedback on key taps
- Like, favorite, and journal states saved locally so they persist
